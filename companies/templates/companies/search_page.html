  • CIN is the short form of "Corporate Identity Number.
  • FCRN is the short form of "Foreign Company Registration Number".
  • LLPIN is the short form of "Limited Liability Partnership Identification Number".

When acompany gets registered in India, be it an Indian Company or a Foreign Company having a place of business in India, it is allotted witha unique code which consists of 21 alphanumeric characters. This is known as ‘Corporate IdentityNumber’, which in short is denoted as ‘CIN’.

Example : CIN of TATA Industries Limited is U44003MH1945PLC004403
